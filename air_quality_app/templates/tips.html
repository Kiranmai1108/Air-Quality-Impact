Stay safe and healthy by understanding how air quality levels impact your daily activities.

Very Low / Low Impact

Air is generally safe. Enjoy outdoor activities freely. Stay hydrated and use sun protection if needed.

Moderate Impact

Sensitive individuals should take care. Children, elderly, or those with respiratory conditions should limit strenuous activities and monitor symptoms.

High Impact

Limit outdoor activities. Everyone should reduce prolonged outdoor exertion. Sensitive groups should avoid outdoor exposure and consider an N95 mask if going out.

Very High Impact

Stay indoors. Keep doors and windows closed, use an air purifier if available, and seek medical advice for any breathing or chest discomfort.
